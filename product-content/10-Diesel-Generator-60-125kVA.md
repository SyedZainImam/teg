## Description
This range of 60-125 kVA diesel generators from leading manufacturers Cummins, Perkins, and FG Wilson provides reliable standby and prime power for commercial, industrial, and institutional applications. These gensets are widely used across hospitals, factories, commercial buildings, construction sites, and telecom installations. Each unit combines a proven diesel engine with a high-quality alternator (typically Stamford/Leroy-Somer) in either canopy-enclosed or open-skid configurations.

Units in this range are versatile enough for small to medium facilities while delivering robust performance in demanding conditions. Used/refurbished generators with documented running hours, service history, and load-bank test certificates offer significant cost savings over new units while providing dependable power backup for critical applications.

## Key Features
- Power output range: 60-125 kVA (48-100 kW)
- Heavy-duty diesel engine (Cummins 4BT/6BT, Perkins 1104/1106 series)
- Quality alternator (Stamford, Leroy-Somer, or equivalent)
- Digital or analog control panel with auto-start capability
- AMF (Automatic Mains Failure) panel option
- Sound-attenuated canopy or open-skid configuration
- Large-capacity fuel tank (8-12 hours runtime)
- Circuit breaker protection
- 3-phase / single-phase output options
- Low fuel consumption at rated load
