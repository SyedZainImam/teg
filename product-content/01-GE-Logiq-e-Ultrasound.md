## Description
The GE Logiq e is a versatile, compact portable ultrasound system designed for point-of-care imaging across multiple clinical settings. Known for its excellent image quality and portability, the Logiq e delivers advanced imaging capabilities in a lightweight, cart-based or portable form factor. It features GE's proprietary CrossXBeam and Coded Harmonic Imaging technologies for superior resolution and tissue differentiation.

Ideal for general radiology, musculoskeletal, vascular, abdominal, and small-parts imaging, the Logiq e is widely used in clinics, emergency departments, and outpatient facilities. Its intuitive user interface and fast boot-up time make it a preferred choice for busy clinical environments requiring rapid bedside diagnostics.

## Key Features
- Compact, lightweight design for point-of-care use
- High-resolution B-mode imaging with CrossXBeam technology
- Coded Harmonic Imaging (CHI) for enhanced tissue contrast
- Color Flow Doppler and Power Doppler modes
- M-Mode and Pulsed Wave Doppler
- 15-inch high-resolution LED display
- Built-in battery option for portable operation
- Multiple transducer compatibility (linear, convex, phased array)
- DICOM connectivity for PACS integration
- USB and network data export capabilities
- Fast boot-up time (under 30 seconds)
