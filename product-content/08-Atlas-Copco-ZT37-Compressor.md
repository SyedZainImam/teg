## Description
The Atlas Copco ZT37 is a 37 kW (50 HP) oil-free rotary tooth air compressor, one step up from the ZT30 in Atlas Copco's renowned ZT series. Certified to ISO 8573-1 Class 0 for oil-free air quality, the ZT37 is engineered for medium-capacity industrial applications in pharmaceutical manufacturing, textile processing, food and beverage production, and general manufacturing where compressed air purity directly impacts product quality.

Featuring the same proven rotary tooth technology with PTFE-coated compression elements, the ZT37 delivers higher air output while maintaining the same compact footprint and low-maintenance design. The integrated Elektronikon controller provides intelligent monitoring, energy management, and easy integration with plant-wide compressed air systems. Available in both fixed-speed and Variable Speed Drive (VSD) variants for maximum energy efficiency.
