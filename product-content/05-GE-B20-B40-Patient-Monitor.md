## Description
The GE B20 and B40 are versatile patient monitors from GE Healthcare's CARESCAPE series, designed for mid-acuity monitoring across a variety of clinical environments. These monitors deliver reliable, continuous vital signs monitoring with an emphasis on ease of use, portability, and clinical flexibility. The B20 serves as a compact bedside monitor for general wards, while the B40 offers expanded capabilities including more advanced parameter modules and a larger display.

Both monitors feature GE's DINAMAP SuperSTAT NIBP technology for fast, accurate blood pressure measurement, and Ohmeda SpO2 or Masimo SET pulse oximetry. They are widely deployed in private hospitals, outpatient surgery centers, and clinics worldwide due to their reliability and low total cost of ownership.

## Key Features
- Multi-parameter monitoring: ECG, SpO2, NIBP, Temperature, Respiration
- GE DINAMAP SuperSTAT NIBP algorithm for rapid BP measurement
- Color TFT display with intuitive navigation
- Nellcor/Masimo/Ohmeda SpO2 technology options
- Arrhythmia analysis with 12-lead ECG review (B40)
- Built-in battery for patient transport
- Flexible mounting options (roll stand, wall mount, shelf)
- GE CARESCAPE network ready
- Configurable alarm limits and profiles
- Compact, lightweight design

## Specifications
- Display: 10.1" (B20) / 12.1" (B40) color TFT
- Weight: ~4.5 kg (B20) / ~5.5 kg (B40)
- Parameters: ECG (3/5-lead), SpO2, NIBP, 2x Temp, Resp, EtCO2 (optional), IBP (optional - B40)
- Battery: Lithium-Ion, up to 2 hours backup
- Alarms: 3-level priority, audible and visual
- Connectivity: Ethernet, USB, GE CARESCAPE Network, HL7
- Power: AC mains + battery
- Trending: Up to 96 hours
