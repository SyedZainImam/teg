## Description
This curated basket of HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) consumables and replacement parts is tailored for pharmaceutical, food-testing, and analytical laboratories. The lot includes essential components required for routine HPLC system maintenance and operation — from analytical columns and guard columns to deuterium and UV lamps, stainless steel and PEEK tubing, fittings, ferrules, inline filters, pump seals, and check valves.

Parts are sourced from leading manufacturers including Agilent, Waters, Phenomenex, Shimadzu, and equivalent OEM suppliers. Most items are new, sealed in original packaging with Certificates of Analysis (CoA) where available. This offering provides labs with a cost-effective way to stock essential consumables, especially in markets where direct manufacturer distribution is limited or expensive.

- HPLC Analytical Columns (C18, C8, HILIC, Phenyl-Hexyl) - Various lengths and particle sizes
