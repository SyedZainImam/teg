## Description
The Atlas Copco ZT45 is a 45 kW (60 HP) oil-free rotary tooth air compressor, the largest model in the compact ZT range. Certified to ISO 8573-1 Class 0, the ZT45 delivers 100% oil-free compressed air for demanding industrial applications. It is purpose-built for larger factories, pharmaceutical plants, and industrial facilities that require high-volume, clean compressed air with zero risk of oil contamination.

The ZT45 combines Atlas Copco's advanced rotary tooth compression technology with robust industrial-grade motors and efficient cooling systems to deliver consistent, reliable performance in continuous-duty applications. Its Elektronikon controller provides comprehensive monitoring, programmable scheduling, and integration with multi-compressor management systems. The VSD variant offers significant energy savings in applications with varying air demand.
