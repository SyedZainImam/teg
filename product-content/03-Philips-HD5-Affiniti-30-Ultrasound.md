## Description
The Philips HD5 and Affiniti 30 are general-purpose ultrasound systems designed for a broad range of clinical applications. The HD5 is a cost-effective shared-service platform offering solid imaging performance for abdominal, vascular, OB/GYN, musculoskeletal, and small-parts examinations. The Affiniti 30 builds on this with Philips' PureWave crystal technology for enhanced image clarity and penetration.

These systems are ideal for small to medium-sized clinics, community hospitals, and practices in secondary cities that need reliable, high-quality diagnostic imaging at an accessible price point. With intuitive interfaces and fast workflow, they allow sonographers to perform efficient examinations across multiple specialties.

## Key Features
- Versatile shared-service platform for multi-specialty use
- PureWave crystal technology (Affiniti 30) for superior image quality
- SonoCT real-time compound imaging
- XRES adaptive image processing for reduced speckle
- Harmonic imaging for improved contrast resolution
- Color Flow and Pulsed Wave Doppler
- Quick-start with streamlined user interface
- Compact cart-based design
- Built-in measurement and reporting tools
- DICOM connectivity for PACS/HIS integration

## Specifications
- Display: 15" LCD monitor (HD5) / 21.5" widescreen (Affiniti 30)
- Weight: ~85 kg (cart-based)
- Imaging Modes: 2D, M-mode, Color Doppler, Power Doppler, PW/CW Doppler
- Transducer Ports: 3 active ports
- Storage: Internal HDD + USB/DVD
- Connectivity: DICOM 3.0, Ethernet, USB
- Power: AC mains
- Frequency Range: 1-12 MHz (probe dependent)
