## Description
This range of 150-250 kVA diesel generator sets from Cummins, Perkins, and FG Wilson delivers medium to heavy-duty power for industrial facilities, large commercial buildings, infrastructure projects, and critical installations. These units feature heavy-duty diesel engines (Cummins 6CTA8.3 / QSB7 or Perkins 1506/2206 series) paired with premium Stamford or Leroy-Somer alternators for reliable, continuous power generation.

Available in weatherproof canopy, containerized, or open-skid configurations, these generators are designed for both standby and continuous operation. They feature advanced digital control systems, automatic transfer switching capability, and comprehensive protection features. Used/refurbished units with verified running hours and load-bank test certificates provide industrial-grade reliability at a fraction of new-unit costs.
