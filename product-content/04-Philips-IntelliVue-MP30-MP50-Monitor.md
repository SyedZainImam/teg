## Description
The Philips IntelliVue MP30 and MP50 are professional-grade patient monitors designed for acute and sub-acute care environments. Part of Philips' widely trusted IntelliVue platform, these monitors provide continuous, real-time vital signs monitoring including ECG, SpO2, NIBP, temperature, and respiratory parameters. The modular design allows flexible configuration to match specific department needs.

The MP30 is optimized for general ward and step-down unit monitoring, while the MP50 adds a larger touchscreen display and enhanced capabilities suitable for ICU, OR, and emergency department use. Both models feature Philips' proven measurement algorithms, intuitive interface, and robust network connectivity for central station integration.

## Key Features
- Multi-parameter monitoring: ECG, SpO2, NIBP, Temperature, Respiration
- High-resolution color TFT touchscreen display
- Modular measurement architecture (MMS/X2 module compatible)
- ST-segment analysis and arrhythmia detection
- Nurse call and alarm management system
- Philips IntelliVue Clinical Network connectivity
- HL7 and DICOM integration for EMR systems
- Battery backup for safe patient transport
- Wall-mount, roll-stand, and bedside mounting options
- Fast discharge/admit workflow

## Specifications
- Display: 10.4" (MP30) / 15" (MP50) color TFT touchscreen
- Weight: ~6.5 kg (MP30) / ~8.5 kg (MP50)
- Parameters: ECG (3/5/12-lead), SpO2, NIBP, 2x Temp, Resp, IBP (optional), CO2 (optional)
- Battery: Li-Ion, up to 90 minutes backup
- Alarms: Visual and audible, configurable priority levels
- Connectivity: Ethernet, IntelliVue Clinical Network, HL7
- Power: AC mains with battery backup
- Data Storage: 72+ hours trending data
