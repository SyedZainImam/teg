## Description
The Atlas Copco ZT30 is a 30 kW (40 HP) oil-free rotary tooth air compressor designed for applications where air purity is critical. Part of Atlas Copco's ZT series, the ZT30 delivers ISO 8573-1 Class 0 certified oil-free air, making it the standard choice for pharmaceutical manufacturing, food and beverage processing, electronics assembly, and textile plants. The oil-free design eliminates any risk of oil contamination in the compressed air supply.

The ZT30 features Atlas Copco's proven rotary tooth compression element with PTFE-coated rotors, ensuring efficient and reliable operation. With integrated cooling, air treatment options, and the Elektronikon controller for intelligent monitoring and energy optimization, the ZT30 delivers consistent performance with low lifecycle costs. Used/refurbished units with documented service history offer exceptional value for industrial buyers.

## Key Features
- ISO 8573-1 Class 0 certified oil-free air
- 30 kW / 40 HP motor
- Rotary tooth compression technology with PTFE-coated elements
- Elektronikon microprocessor controller with graphic display

- Low noise levels (~70 dB(A))
