## Description
The GE Voluson i is a premium portable ultrasound system specifically engineered for women's health imaging, with a strong focus on obstetrics and gynecology. Part of GE's renowned Voluson series, the Voluson i delivers exceptional 2D, 3D, and 4D imaging in a compact, portable package. It features GE's advanced Volume Contrast Imaging (VCI) and SonoRenderlive technologies for stunning volumetric renderings of fetal anatomy.

This system is the go-to choice for OB/GYN clinics, maternity hospitals, and fertility centers that need high-quality imaging without the footprint of a full-size console. Its dedicated OB measurement packages, automated follicle tracking, and advanced Doppler capabilities make it a comprehensive solution for prenatal care and gynecological diagnostics.

## Key Features
- Specialized OB/GYN imaging with dedicated presets and measurement packages
- Real-time 3D/4D imaging with SonoRenderlive technology
- Volume Contrast Imaging (VCI) for enhanced visualization
- SonoVCAD (Volume Computer-Aided Diagnosis) for automated measurements
- Automated Follicle Tracking (SonoAVC follicle)
- HD-Flow and Color Doppler imaging
- CrossXBeam CRI technology for compound resolution
- Portable design with optional trolley setup
- 15-inch flat-panel LED display
- Speckle Reduction Imaging (SRI) for cleaner images
- DICOM 3.0 connectivity

## Specifications
- Display: 15" LED flat-panel
- Weight: ~6.3 kg (console only); ~25 kg (with trolley)
- Imaging Modes: 2D, 3D, 4D, M-mode, Color Doppler, Power Doppler, PW Doppler
- Transducer Ports: 2 active probe connectors
- Storage: Built-in HDD + USB
- Connectivity: DICOM, Ethernet, USB, optional Wi-Fi
- Power: AC mains with optional battery
- Frequency Range: 2-18 MHz (probe dependent)
